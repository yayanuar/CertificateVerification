Process

How certificate verification works

Every certificate is hashed and stored immutably on-chain. Verification is instant and requires no trusted third party.

01 / 04

Issue Certificate

An institution creates a certificate record with the recipient's details and metadata.

02 / 04

Hash & Sign

The data is cryptographically hashed (SHA-256) and signed with the institution's private key.

03 / 04

Record On-Chain

The hash is written to the blockchain smart contract — permanent and tamper-evident.

04 / 04

Verify Instantly

Anyone can verify authenticity by submitting the certificate ID or hash — no login required.

Why CertChain

Built for trust at every step

Every design decision prioritises security, accessibility, and institutional confidence.

Tamper-Proof Records

Once recorded on-chain, a certificate hash cannot be altered. Any modification invalidates the record immediately.

Instant Verification

Verification takes under 3 seconds. Employers and institutions get a definitive answer without contacting anyone.

No Intermediaries

Verification is done directly against the blockchain. No registrar calls, no email requests, no waiting weeks.

Private Key Signing

Each certificate is cryptographically signed by the issuing institution, proving authorship and authority.

Low Issuance Cost

Gas-optimised smart contracts keep on-chain recording affordable even at high institutional volumes.

Full Audit Trail

Every issuance, revocation, and lookup is logged on-chain. Complete, immutable, and publicly auditable.
